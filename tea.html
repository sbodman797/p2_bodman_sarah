The Benefits of a Leaf.

THE MEANING OF TEA

Insight to Haley's Thoughts

TEA IS STRONGER THAN WE THINK.

There are hundreds of different types of teas. Each hold their own benefit. Whether it be to calm you to sleep or give you energy, these herbs can help. Take it hot or cold. Just don't sip a cold, hot tea.

Black Tea

The most "basic". The most common. It is known to make you more alert and give you energy. All the while, it reduces stress by targeting those pesky hormones. It is high in caffeine and is the basis for other flavored teas. It is good for the heart and lungs. Typically hot.

Chamomile Tea

Nature's natural sleeping aid. It is caffeine free and used for relaxation. Thus, it is one of the most popular teas. It can help your overall health to fight colds and become refreshed. Typically hot.

Green Tea

Made from steamed tea leaves and filled with antioxidants. Think, green means good. Good for those with high cholesterol, weight management, or high blood sugar. Drink it cold or hot.

Chai Tea

The coffee of tea. The base is black tea. However, it is filled with spices, cream, and sugar or honey. Most commonly known as a spiced tea for its cinnamon flavor. Good for energy and happiness. Typically hot.
